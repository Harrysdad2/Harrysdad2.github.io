# Small Obelisk

I made this obelisk in late 2025. This was shipped to Aberdeen and then taken to Ireland as a Christmas gift.

Only about 600mm tall, the wood was shaped like a sail. Intended to be kept indoors, it was finished with an antique wood wax.
